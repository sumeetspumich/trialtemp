First Gen Students

About First-Generation Student Support

First-Generation students are pioneers within their families, being the first to attend college. We understand the unique challenges and experiences that come with being a First-Gen student, and we are here to help you navigate this new environment.

Our office provides dedicated services such as mentoring programs, academic workshops, and financial aid guidance tailored specifically for First-Generation students.

Resources for First-Gen Students

We offer a variety of resources to help you succeed academically and personally:

  • Academic Advising and Tutoring
  • Mental Health and Wellness Support
  • Financial Aid and Scholarship Guidance
  • Career Development Workshops
  • Student Organizations and Networking Events

Support Programs

First-Generation Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program pairs First-Generation students with faculty, staff, or upper-level students who can provide guidance, support, and encouragement throughout the academic year. This program aims to foster a sense of belonging and community.

Learn more about our workshops

First-Generation Student Network

Join the First-Generation Student Network to connect with peers who share similar experiences. This network hosts regular meetings, social events, and study sessions to help you build lasting relationships and succeed academically.
